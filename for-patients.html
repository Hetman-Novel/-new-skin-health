Why is it important to do weekly self-test?

The way you brush your teeth 2 times per day, skin self-examinations should be included in the weekly routine.Weekly self-checkups are crucial for maintaining skin health, maintaining existing conditions, and preventive diagnostics of diseases.

Skin diseases can easily missed in the early stages. Performing weekly self-tests will help you to catch the anomaly early on and treat it quicker and more efficiently.
